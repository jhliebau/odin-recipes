Healthy Cinnamon Toast Crunch

Description

Elevate your breakfast game with this nutritious twist on a classic favorite. Start by gently crushing heritage flakes in a bowl, ensuring they maintain some texture and crunch. Drizzle the flakes with local honey and sprinkle generously with cinnamon, mixing well to evenly coat. Serve the cinnamon-coated flakes in a bowl, and pour over some rich and creamy Fairlife whole milk. Each spoonful offers a delightful crunch, the natural sweetness of honey, and a warm, comforting hint of cinnamon. This wholesome cereal is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients to start your day off right. Enjoy a bowl of this homemade cinnamon toast crunch and savor every crunchy, flavorful bite

Ingredients

  • Local honey
  • Hertiage Flakes Cereal
  • cinnamon
  • Fairlife Whole Milk
