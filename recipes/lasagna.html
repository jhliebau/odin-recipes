Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this mouthwatering lasagna recipe. Layers of perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, rich and savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey melted mozzarella come together to create a dish that's sure to satisfy your cravings. Start by preparing a flavorful meat sauce with ground beef, onions, garlic, and crushed tomatoes, seasoned to perfection with Italian herbs and spices. Next, layer the noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish, repeating until all the ingredients are used up. Finish with a generous sprinkling of Parmesan cheese for an extra layer of flavor. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the noodles are tender. Serve hot and enjoy the irresistible flavors of this classic lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic crushed
